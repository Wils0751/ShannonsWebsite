MeetCute Madlib

Cross Platform Mobile Application

Meetcute Madlib was a final project for one of my courses at Algonquin College. Using a form a user is to generate a profile and the app will then create a QR Code for you. Other users with the same app are able to scan your QR code and than it will be added to your Contacts. You are able to see and view the information of all the users and contacts that you have scanned in your contact page. Shannon has learned how to develop with Cross platform using Cordova. She is currently learning Angular Javascript and Ionic in school.

  • Date: April 2016
  • Client: Algonquin College
  • Category: Cross Platform Applications
